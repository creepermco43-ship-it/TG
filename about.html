Who We Are

A Professional Gaming Organization

Team TG is not just a gaming clan — it's a movement. Founded with the vision of creating a professional, disciplined, and respected gaming organization in the Indian gaming scene, Team TG has grown to compete across multiple titles and host some of the most talented content creators and competitive players in the community.

We believe that gaming is more than entertainment — it's a profession, an art, and a lifestyle. Our members represent the highest standards of sportsmanship, creativity, and dedication that the gaming world demands.
